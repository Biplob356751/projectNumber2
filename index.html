Buy on Pancakeswap

Step 1: Install Metamask for Chrome, Android or iOS

Go to App Store or Play Store install the Metamask app. Follow the steps from their guide to set up a wallet.

Step 2: Send BSC (Binance Smart chain) to Metamask

Set up your wallet to receive and send payments on the BSC (Binance Smart chain) network. Set up a new network with the following data: Name: MAINNET BSC, URL RPC: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/ ID: 56 SYMBOL: BNB URL: https://bscscan.com/.

Step 3: Connect to Pancakeswap

Go to https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/ and click the “Use Pancakeswap” button. When Metamask asks for your signature, go ahead and sign it.

Step 4: Swap your BSC for HASHI INU

Now you can swap your BSC for HASHI INU. Set your slippage to 5-7% and buy an even amount of HASHI INU. Add your desired BSC amount and the correct HASHI INU amount will be calculated. Click on the “Swap” button to finalize the trade. make sure to set “fast transaction speed” in Metamask.
